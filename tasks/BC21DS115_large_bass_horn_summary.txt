BUILD NOTES:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  • This is a MASSIVE horn (2.5m diameter × 4.5m long)
  • Consider folding to fit in practical cabinet
  • No rear chamber = driver needs protection below Fs
  • Use high-pass filter at 35-40 Hz for driver safety
  • Build with rigid materials (18mm+ plywood)
  • Seal all joints airtight
  • Driver mounting: rear-loaded (horn in front of driver)
